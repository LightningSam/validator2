Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify a certificate?

Simply upload your certificate file in PDF, JPG, or PNG format. Our system will automatically extract information and verify it against institutional records and blockchain signatures.

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF, JPG, JPEG, and PNG files up to 10MB in size. For best results, ensure your certificate is clearly visible and not blurred.

How secure is the verification process?

All verifications are processed using blockchain technology and AI-powered fraud detection. Your documents are encrypted and not stored permanently on our servers.
